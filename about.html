About Us

Jayapura Mall is a shopping center located on Jl. Sam Ratulangi APO, Ex. Bhayangkara, Kec. North Jayapura, Papua, Indonesia.

Jayapura Mall is a shopping center located in the heart of Jayapura City on Jl. APO Sam Ratulangi, Kelurahan Bhayangkara, Kecamatan Jayapura Utara, Jayapura Municipality, Papua, Indonesia. Jayapura Mall operates on November 1, 2012. Jayapura Mall was built by PT. Modern Widya Tehnical and its management is carried out by PT. Papua International Mall.

Jayapura Mall with the concept of Lease Mall (Lease), consisting of 5 floors and 1 Mezzanine with the concept of “4 in 1 Shopping Destinations” The location of Jayapura Mall faces Papua Police, Cenderawasih Sports Building and not far from Swiss Belhotel Papua.

Jayapura Mall is a spending center that has 6 floors with an area of ​​35,0074 m2 and clean area that can be rented out 28,000 m2, not including the parking area floor.

Jayapura Mall is equipped with a travelator and elevator to make it easy for visitors to get around on each floor. Jayapura Mall has an average annual number of visitors of 275 thousand people.

Some of the main tenants are Matahari Dept. Store, Hypermarket (Hypermart), Cinema XXI and Premiere, Entertainment Center (Amazone Game Center). On the 3rd floor there is a Food Court area and also others such as: C & F Perfumery, Camera and Optics, Logo, Rodeo, Eprise, Story I, Planet Surf, Fashion Boutique, Samsung Indonesia, Sports Station, Natasha Skin Care, Yopie Salon, buy , JCO Donuts, Breadtalk, Solaria, Excelso, Warung Pojok Inul Vizta Family KTV.
